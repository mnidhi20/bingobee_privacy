# BingoBEE Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

BingoBEE ("we", "our", or "us") is a kids bingo app designed for children and families. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the app and how it is used.

## Children and Age Audience

BingoBEE is intended for children under 13 and is configured as a child-directed app.

## Information We Collect

We do not ask children to create accounts and we do not collect direct personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or home address in the app.

The app may use third-party services that process limited technical information needed to operate purchases and ads:

- Device and app information needed for app functionality.
- Purchase-related status from the app stores to validate and restore purchases.
- Ad request and delivery data through Google AdMob (configured for child-directed treatment and non-personalized/age-appropriate ad serving).

## In-App Purchases

BingoBEE offers optional in-app purchases:

- `bingo_new_set_once` (one-time consumable)
- `bingo_monthly_sets` (auto-renewable subscription)

Purchases are processed by Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

## Advertising (AdMob)

BingoBEE uses Google AdMob banner ads in limited app areas. Interstitial and rewarded ads are not used in gameplay flows.

For child safety:

- Child-directed treatment is enabled.
- Under age of consent treatment is enabled.
- Max ad content rating is set to "G".
- Ads are intended to be non-personalized and age-appropriate.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited data may be shared with service providers (such as Apple, Google Play Billing, and Google AdMob) only to provide core app features such as billing and ad delivery.

## Data Retention

We do not maintain user account profiles in the app. Purchase history and transaction records are handled by the platform providers (Apple/Google) according to their policies.

## Your Choices

- You can avoid optional purchases by not completing purchase prompts.
- You can manage subscriptions in your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings.

## Security

We use reasonable measures and trusted platform services to support app security. No system can guarantee absolute security.

## International Users

Your information may be processed by Apple, Google, or their service providers in accordance with their global infrastructure and policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will be revised when changes are made.
