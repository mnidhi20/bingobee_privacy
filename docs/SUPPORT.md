# BingoBEE Support

Thank you for using **BingoBEE**, a fun bingo app for kids and families.

## How to get help

If you have questions, need help with the app, or want to report a problem, contact us by email:

**Email:** basics.learn.ai@gmail.com

We try to respond within **2–3 business days**.

## Common topics

### Printing bingo cards
- Open a game from the home screen.
- Tap **Print Bingo Cards**.
- Select the cards you want, then print or share the PDF.

### Bingo caller
- Open a game → **Bingo Caller**.
- In **Settings**, choose automatic calls or tap-to-call, voice style, and time between calls.

### New card sets (in-app purchases)
- The first **10 cards per category** are free.
- **New Set** unlocks 10 new printable cards for one category (one-time purchase).
- **Monthly Set Pass** allows up to **2 new sets per day** across categories while subscribed.
- Use **Restore Purchases** in the paywall or Settings if you reinstall the app or switch devices.

### Subscriptions
- Subscriptions are managed by **Apple App Store** or **Google Play**.
- To cancel or change a subscription, use your device’s store account settings.
- Charges and refunds follow Apple/Google store policies.

### Ads
- BingoBEE shows **banner ads** only on some screens (not during caller or print gameplay).
- The app is configured for **child-directed**, age-appropriate advertising.
